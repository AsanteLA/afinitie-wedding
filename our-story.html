The Beginning

Our Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is our favourite.

Some stories begin with a grand gesture. Ours began with a friendship, a goodbye that wasn't forever, and a surprise reunion in a hallway at BYU — followed by a very anxious text message and a very long five minutes of watching the typing indicator.

Our Journey

Chapter by Chapter

2024

First Connection

Abbie and Asante first crossed paths through a mutual connection — and even then, something clicked. They became fast friends, the kind who could talk for hours and always leave the conversation wanting more.

2025

A Respectful Goodbye

Life shifted and circumstance pulled them apart. Abbie, being the loyal and gracious person she is, stepped back — and Asante respected that too. They went their separate ways, but the friendship never really left either of them.

Fall 2025

The Surprise Reunion

Asante was calling students out at BYU's Information Systems orientation to get their professional headshots taken — the same program he had quietly coached Abbie through applying for. He had no idea she'd gotten in. Then he stumbled onto her team, and there she was. He gave her grief, she laughed it off, and just like that, they were back.

Fall 2025

Pies with Professors

At an IS program event, Asante watched Abbie light up a room full of professors and classmates and thought: I need to do more things with this person. He went home and sent the text. She started typing. Then kept typing. Five of the longest minutes of his life. Then: "I want to spend more time with you too."

Nov 2025

Making It Official

What started as hangouts and late conversations became something neither of them could ignore. After a BYU football game — one Abbie came to straight off a long family trip from St. George — they ordered wings and ended up at Asante's place. On November 15th, 2025, over wings, they made it official. Everything has been amazing ever since.

Sep 2026

Forever Starts Here

And now, surrounded by the people they love most, Abbie and Asante say yes to a lifetime together on September 15th, 2026. We're so glad you're here to witness it.
