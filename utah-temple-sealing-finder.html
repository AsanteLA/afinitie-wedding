🏛 Utah Temple Sealing Finder

Find available sealing appointments across all Utah temples — September 2026

⚙️ Step 1 — Install the bookmarklet

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar (show it with Cmd+Shift+B if hidden):

🏛 Utah Sealing Finder

Or right-click → "Bookmark this link"

Note: The bookmarklet only runs on tos.churchofjesuschrist.org while you're logged in — it uses your existing session, so no passwords are ever touched or stored.

🚀 Step 2 — Use it

  1. Go to tos.churchofjesuschrist.org and sign in with your Church Account.
  2. Click the "🏛 Utah Sealing Finder" bookmark — a panel appears on the right side.
  3. In the scheduler, click any Utah temple, select Sealing as the ordinance, then pick any date in September 2026.
  4. The panel detects the API automatically and searches all Utah temples for the entire month of September.
  5. Green dates have openings. Click any date to see which temples have time and when.

The tool discovers temple IDs automatically from the scheduling site — no hard-coded IDs.

❓ Troubleshooting

Panel doesn't appear: Make sure you're on tos.churchofjesuschrist.org (not the main churchofjesuschrist.org) and signed in.
No results after navigating: Try clicking a specific temple, choosing Sealing, then selecting a September date — the panel needs to see at least one availability API call.
Click the bookmark again to close and reset the panel.
