For Our Guests

About the Temple Sealing

We want everyone to feel informed and comfortable on our big day. Here's a little background on what a temple sealing is and what to expect.

What is a temple sealing?

As members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, Asante and Abbie will be married — or "sealed" — in a sacred ceremony performed inside the temple. The sealing binds them together not just for this life, but for eternity.

Who can attend the sealing?

Temple sealings are open to members of the Church who hold a current temple recommend. We understand this may mean some of our beloved family and friends cannot be inside, and we are so grateful for your love and support regardless.

What should I do outside?

All guests are warmly invited to gather on the beautiful temple grounds to greet us as we exit. This is one of our favourite moments — seeing everyone waiting for us outside. Come celebrate with us there!

What should I wear?

Temple grounds call for modest, respectful attire. Think Sunday best — dresses, suits, or smart casual. Our wedding colors are navy, burgundy, and gold if you'd like to coordinate!
