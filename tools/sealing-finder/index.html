Or right-click → "Bookmark this link"

Note: The bookmarklet only runs on the temple scheduling site while you're logged in — it uses your existing session. No passwords are touched or stored.

🚀 Step 3 — Use it

  1. Go to tos.churchofjesuschrist.org and sign in with your Church Account.
  2. Click the "🏛 Utah Sealing Finder" bookmark — a panel appears on the right.
  3. In the scheduler, pick any Utah temple, choose Living Sealing (under living ordinances), then click any date inside your range.
  4. The panel detects the API automatically and searches all Utah temples across your whole date range for living sealing openings.
  5. Green dates have openings. Click any date to see which temples have times, and when.

❓ Troubleshooting

Panel doesn't appear: Make sure you're on tos.churchofjesuschrist.org and signed in.
Stuck on "Waiting for API": Click a specific temple, choose Living Sealing, then click a date inside your range — the panel needs to see one availability call to learn the API.
Wrong dates searched: The dates are baked into the bookmark. Change them above, then re-drag the button to replace the old bookmark.
Click the bookmark again to close and reset the panel.
